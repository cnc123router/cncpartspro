BoardBond

Looking for an easy way to hold down your materials without dealing with clamps or messy adhesives? BoardBond is a non-adhesive bonding agent that delivers significant holding strength while leaving absolutely no residue. Each piece is reusable — simply wash in cold water with no detergent and it's ready to go again.

Specifications
Roll Length14 feet
Adhesive TypeNon-Adhesive (no residue)
ReusableYes — wash in cold water, no detergent
Holding StrengthUltra Strong
ApplicationCNC spoilboard material hold-down
How to use: Cut to desired size, apply to the underside of your material, and press onto the spoilboard. Reuse multiple times by washing in cold water.
