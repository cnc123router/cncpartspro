What We Cover

We've Seen Every Problem.
We Know How to Fix It.

Mach3 / Mach4 Setup

Motor tuning, axis configuration, homing switches, spindle control, toolpath validation. We'll get your controller talking to your machine correctly.

Vector File Preparation

DXF, SVG, AI — we take your raw design files and prep them for cutting. Clean nodes, proper layers, correct scale, and the right output format for your CAM software.

Toolpath & CAM Troubleshooting

VCarve, Aspire, Fusion 360, RhinoCAM — wrong cut depth, missed pockets, bad tool selection. We'll review your toolpaths and fix what's causing bad cuts.

Remote Desktop Support

Via AnyDesk or TeamViewer. We connect directly to your machine's control computer, see exactly what you see, and fix it live — no shipping, no waiting.

New Operator Training

Just hired someone to run the machine? We'll walk them through the full workflow — from loading a file to zeroing the tool to running a safe first cut.

Computer Bundle Configuration

Just received a Computer Bundle or Plug-and-Play Mini Computer? We'll do a live walk-through, verify all settings are dialed in, and make sure your first cut goes smoothly.
